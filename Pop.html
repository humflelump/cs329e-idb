Pop Music

Pop music is sometimes confused with popular music, or music that many people like, but it is accepted as its own style of music best described as professional music with influences of folk and rock that includes catchy repeated choruses and hooks. It appeals in large part to teenagers and often takes the form of songs released as singles. Originated in the United States and United Kingdom.
