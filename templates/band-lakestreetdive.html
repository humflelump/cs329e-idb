Lake Street Dive

Lake Street Dive is a multi-genre band based in Boston, Massachusetts. The band consists of former classmates of the New England Conservatory of Music. The band was named after a street in the hometown of Mike Olson, the guitarist, with many dive bars in Minneapolis, Minnesota. Each member of the band plays at least one instruments. Some have received classical music training or had musician parents. The band began as country band but was abandoned for desire to try something new. Nearly six years after forming the band, they finally signed a contract with a record label. The band gained attention by posting a video of a cover of Jackson 5 song “I Want You Back” on a Brighton, Massachusetts street corner. They have made many appearances on daytime and late night shows such as The Late Show with David Letterman, The Colbert Report and Ellen DeGeneres Show. The band draws influence from The Beatles, Motown, Fleetwood Mac, classic pop, rock and roll, soul from the sixties and swing era jazz.
