Rock Music

Rock music has its deepest roots in rock and roll but draws influence from every type of music, especially blues, country, folk, and jazz music. It is characterized by electric guitar, drums, bass, and one or more singers. It has a well defined beat and can touch on any subject but is often associated with having fun, as the saying goes "sex, drugs, and rock and roll". Originated in the United States.
